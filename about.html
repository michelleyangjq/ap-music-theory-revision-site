A message from the creator

👋 Welcome and hi! My name is Michelle, a current CS and Music undergrad, and I took AP Music Theory as a sophomore in high school.

💻 I know that, while constantly undermined, AP Music Theory is a hard subject with not enough resources to go around. Hence, I built this site with hopes to help fellow students out there prepare for their exams and learn music theory. All notes were made by me while I was a TA tutoring AP Music Theory. I also included two pages with my tips for approaching dictation and sightsinging.

🎶 Hopefully this site offers some help and guidance. Good luck on your studies and have fun!

- Michelle
